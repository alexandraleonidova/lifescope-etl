How do I sign up or log in?

LifeScope demonstrates the power and flexibility of the BitScoop platform by allowing you to sign up or log in using any of the services from which you can retrieve data.

Just click on any service, Connect to it, and authorize LifeScope to access the data it requests.

This Connection is used for both logging you in and retrieving data, hence why it may ask for permissions that don't seem relevant for just login.

Why do I need to re-authorize permissions when logging in?

If you're logged out, we make a new Connection to that service since we don't know whether one already exists.

The new Connection is automatically deleted once we can determine that you already had a Connection.

Any permissions you may or may not grant on that discarded Connection are not carried over to the existing one.

Why don't all my Connections appear in the same account?

In order to associate multiple Connections with the same LifeScope account, you must be logged in and then Connect from the /providers page.

If you sign up using one service, log out, then sign up using a different service, we will have ended up creating two separate LifeScope accounts for you.
